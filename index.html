Who We Are

Connecting the world through sustainable sourcing and innovative technology

Brutus Enterprise Solutions is a sustainable sourcing and technology company providing global access to high-quality equipment, parts, and energy solutions. Through a trusted international supply network spanning the United States, the United Kingdom, China, and select global markets, we help individuals and businesses source reliable products at competitive prices.

Our offerings span multiple sectors, including automotive parts, solar and HVAC equipment, EV charging solutions, energy storage systems, and household appliances. In addition to third-party sourcing, we design and distribute our own branded product lines, including air conditioning systems, EV chargers, and solar PV inverters and battery solutions.
